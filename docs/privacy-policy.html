Smart Story AI ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by Good Day Innovation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Smart Story AI mobile application and website (bearrytales.datainfo.cloud).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture provided by those services.
  • Content you create — Story topics, settings, and generated stories are stored so you can view and manage them later.
  • Usage data — Basic usage events (e.g. story views) are recorded to improve the service. No third-party analytics SDK is embedded.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and store your personalised stories.
  • To associate generated content with your account so it is accessible across devices.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — identity verification. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In — identity verification. See Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API — AI text generation for stories. Prompts may be processed by Google's servers.
  • Microsoft Edge TTS — text-to-speech audio generation.

4. Children's Privacy

Smart Story AI is designed to help create stories for children, but the app itself is intended to be used by parents, educators, or adults on behalf of children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers located in Thailand. We use industry-standard security measures (HTTPS/TLS, hashed credentials) to protect your information. No system is 100% secure; use the app at your own discretion.

6. Data Retention

We keep your account and stories for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Your Rights

  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by stopping use of the app.
